La Boulangerie

Step inside our cozy, rustic boulangerie and you instantly feel a connection with the old-world charms of France. From rows of wicker baskets holding our freshly baked bread to the glass-encased display of sugary pastries, La Boulangerie's ambience and design are pure French.
